Welcome to CUAA!

Chandigarh University Alumni Association (CUAA) is rooted in values to foster alumni relations and is driven to uplift both present and future alumnus through continuous support to build a social, knowledgeable and motivational capital for Chandigarh University and its Alumni.

40,000+

Yes, you heard it right! We have more than 40,000 CUians associated with Chandigarh University Alumni Association (CUAA)

Alumni Meets

We organize alumni meets across the globe where you get an opportunity to relive your college days and learn about how your peers are doing.
Get ready to grab the chance to walk down memory lane, cherish the old days, reconnect with your old classmates, and make new friends.

Careers

We also keep you informed about the career options available for you inside and outside Chandigarh University. So stay connected with us.
